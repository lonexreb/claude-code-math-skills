That ℚ does not have the least-upper-bound property is one of the most important reasons we work with ℝ in analysis. The set ℚ is just fine for algebraists. But us analysts require the least-upper-bound property to do any work. We also require our real numbers to have many algebraic properties. In particular, we require that they be a field.
