## **7.2.2 Connected sets**

Let us generalize the idea of an interval to general metric spaces. One of the main features of an interval in ℝ is that it is connected—that we can continuously move from one point of it to another point without jumping. For example, in ℝ we usually study functions on intervals, and in more general metric spaces we usually study functions on connected sets.
